Can Windscreen Washer Fail MOT?

Yes, faulty windscreen washers can fail an MOT in the UK if they do not operate correctly or cannot clean the windscreen effectively. Washers are important because they help maintain clear driver visibility.

Quick answer

If your washer system does not spray fluid onto the windscreen, sprays weakly, leaks badly or cannot help clear the screen, it may fail an MOT.

A simple issue such as an empty washer bottle is often easy to fix before the test.

Common reasons windscreen washers fail MOT

Empty washer bottle

No fluid available to clean the windscreen.

Blocked washer jets

Jets cannot spray correctly onto the glass.

Failed washer pump

Pump motor may stop working or become weak.

Leaking hose

Split pipes can leak fluid under the bonnet.

Electrical fault

Fuse, relay, wiring or switch problems.

Poor spray pattern

Fluid misses the glass or only trickles out.

What to check before your MOT

1. Fill washer fluid

Top up the bottle with proper screenwash mixture.

2. Test spray

Check fluid reaches the windscreen properly.

3. Listen for pump

If no sound is heard, the pump or fuse may be faulty.

4. Check jets

Blocked jets can often be cleaned carefully.

5. Look for leaks

Check for fluid leaking under bonnet or near wheels.

6. Check wipers too

Washers and wipers are closely linked in MOT checks.

How to fix windscreen washer faults

Many washer faults are quick and inexpensive to fix. Start with the simplest causes before replacing parts.

  • ✓Top up washer fluid and use proper screenwash.
  • ✓Clean blocked jets with care.
  • ✓Replace blown fuse if confirmed faulty.
  • ✓Repair split hoses or loose connectors.
  • ✓Replace weak or failed washer pump.

Why washers matter for MOT

Washer systems help remove dirt, salt, insects and road spray so the driver can see clearly. Poor visibility can become dangerous quickly, especially in winter or wet weather.

That is why washers are checked alongside wipers and general visibility items.

Typical UK repair costs

Screenwash top-up

Low cost.

Jet cleaning

Often low cost or DIY.

Pump replacement

Often modest cost depending on vehicle.

Costs vary depending on vehicle type, access and whether hoses or wiring also need repair.

Frequently asked questions

Will empty washer fluid fail MOT?

It can if the washers cannot operate properly.

Can blocked jets fail MOT?

Yes, if they stop fluid reaching the windscreen effectively.

Do rear washers matter?

Rear washer checks can depend on vehicle design and fitted equipment.

Can I fix it myself?

Many simple issues such as topping up fluid or cleaning jets are easy to fix.

Can a washer pump fail suddenly?

Yes, pumps can fail with age or after running dry.

Best step before MOT?

Test washers and wipers before the appointment.
