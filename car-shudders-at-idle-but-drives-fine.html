Car Shudders at Idle But Drives Fine

If your car shudders at idle but drives fine once moving, the issue is often related to engine mounts, idle control, minor misfires or air/fuel balance problems.

Quick answer

Because the problem disappears while driving, the fault often shows most clearly at low rpm when engine movement and idle smoothness matter most.

Many causes are fixable without major engine work.

Is it serious?

Often minor

Especially if performance is normal.

Monitor worsening

If vibration increases, inspect soon.

Warning lights?

Needs faster diagnosis.

Noise present?

Mount or accessory issue possible.

Rough idle only

Often manageable initially.

Persistent issue

Do not ignore for months.

Common causes

  • 1Worn engine mounts
  • 2Dirty throttle body
  • 3Minor ignition misfire
  • 4Vacuum leak
  • 5Low idle speed control issue

What to check next

Notice whether vibration changes with air conditioning on, in gear, or when cold versus warm. Those clues help diagnosis.

A diagnostic scan and visual inspection are often worthwhile.
