About the site

About Motor Vehicle Expert

Motor Vehicle Expert is a UK-focused automotive information website created to help drivers understand car problems, warning lights, servicing, MOT issues, repair costs, speed limits and used car buying decisions in a clear and practical way.

Browse car guides Visit diagnostics hub
Purpose

Practical help for everyday UK drivers

Motor Vehicle Expert is built for normal drivers who want straightforward answers before spending money, booking repairs or buying a used car.

The site focuses on common real-world questions such as what a warning light means, whether a fault is urgent, what a repair might involve, how MOT issues work and what to check before buying a vehicle.

Important note

The information on this website is general guidance only. It is not a replacement for professional vehicle inspection, diagnosis, repair or legal advice.
