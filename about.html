About Motor Vehicle Expert

Motor Vehicle Expert is a UK-focused car advice website built to help everyday drivers understand vehicle faults, warning lights, MOT issues, servicing, maintenance, repair costs and used car buying decisions in plain, practical language.

Purpose

Practical car advice for real UK drivers

Most drivers do not need confusing workshop jargon. They need to know what a symptom might mean, how serious it could be, what to check first and when it is time to get professional diagnosis.

Motor Vehicle Expert is designed for those real-world moments: a warning light appears, the car starts making a noise, the MOT is due, a repair quote feels expensive, or you are checking a used car before buying.

Trust and safety

How to use the information safely

Car symptoms can have more than one cause. For example, a warning light, noise, smell or vibration may point to a simple issue or something more serious depending on the vehicle, mileage, service history and driving conditions.

Use the guides to understand likely causes and sensible checks, but do not ignore serious symptoms such as oil warning lights, brake warning lights, overheating, smoke, fuel smells, coolant loss or unsafe braking.

If the vehicle feels unsafe, stop driving and arrange professional help.
