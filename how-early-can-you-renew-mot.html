How Early Can You Renew MOT?

In the UK, you can usually renew your MOT up to one month minus a day before the current MOT expires and often keep the same renewal date pattern.

Quick answer

Many drivers book within the final month before expiry to keep continuity and avoid accidentally letting the MOT lapse.

Always confirm your exact expiry date first.

What if you renew too early?

If you test much earlier than the final month, the next expiry timing may effectively move forward, shortening the current cycle.

That is why many owners wait until the eligible early renewal window.

Booking tips

Choose a reputable MOT centre, fix advisories early and combine the booking with servicing if helpful.
