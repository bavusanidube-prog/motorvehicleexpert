How Much Does Clutch Replacement Cost in the UK?

Quick answer

Clutch replacement cost in the UK can vary widely depending on the make, model, labour time, parts quality and whether related components such as the flywheel or hydraulic parts also need replacement.

What affects clutch replacement cost

  • ✓Vehicle make and model
  • ✓How long the job takes
  • ✓Whether the flywheel also needs attention
  • ✓Parts quality
  • ✓Garage labour rates

Why quotes vary so much

Some cars have simple access, while others require far more labour to remove the gearbox and replace the clutch assembly.

What should be included in a proper quote

  • ✓Clutch kit
  • ✓Labour
  • ✓Any flywheel advice if relevant
  • ✓Hydraulic component advice if needed
  • ✓VAT and total price clarity

Best advice before spending money

Make sure the fault really is clutch-related. Not every poor gear change or drivability issue means the clutch itself has failed.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does every clutch job include a flywheel?

No. It depends on the vehicle and flywheel condition.

Can I keep driving with a worn clutch?

Only cautiously and for as little as possible if symptoms are worsening.

Should I choose the cheapest quote?

Not automatically. Clarity, diagnosis and parts quality matter as much as headline price.
