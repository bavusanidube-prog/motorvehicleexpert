Can You Sell a Car Without MOT?

Yes, you can usually sell a car without MOT in the UK, but it often reduces buyer confidence and sale value. Many buyers prefer vehicles with a current MOT certificate.

Quick answer

Selling without MOT is usually possible, but expect more questions, lower offers and fewer interested buyers.

A fresh MOT can make selling easier and faster.

Best tips when selling

Be clear about why there is no MOT, list known faults honestly and gather service receipts.

Sometimes paying for an MOT first can increase sale price more than the test cost.

What buyers usually check

Mileage history, MOT advisories, rust, tyres, warning lights, clutch feel and service evidence are common checks.
