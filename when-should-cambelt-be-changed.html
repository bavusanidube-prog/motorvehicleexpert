When Should a Cambelt Be Changed?

Cambelt replacement intervals vary by vehicle, but ignoring them can be expensive because a failed cambelt can cause major engine damage on many engines.

Quick answer

Cambelt replacement intervals vary by vehicle, but ignoring them can be expensive because a failed cambelt can cause major engine damage on many engines.

Why cambelt timing matters

The cambelt keeps engine timing in sync.

Why there is no single replacement rule

That is why checking the correct interval for the exact car matters.

What buyers should ask

  • ✓Has the cambelt been replaced?
  • ✓Is there an invoice or proof?
  • ✓Was the water pump changed at the same time if recommended?

Best practical advice

If cambelt history is unknown and the car is due or overdue, budget for replacement rather than guessing.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is mileage or age more important?

Both matter. The belt can age as well as wear with mileage.

Can a cambelt look fine and still be due?

Yes. Visual appearance is not a reliable guide to service interval.

Should I change the water pump too?

Often yes if it is part of the same job or recommended for the vehicle.
