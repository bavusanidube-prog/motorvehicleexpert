Seasonal checks worth doing

Before winter

Battery health, heater performance, tyres and wipers matter most.

Before summer

Cooling system, coolant level and air conditioning checks are useful.

Before long trips

Check fluids, tyres, lights and recovery equipment.

Before MOT

Inspect tyres, lights, washers and warning lights first.

Warning signs not to ignore

  • !New warning light appears
  • !Grinding brakes or vibration
  • !Coolant loss or overheating
  • !Repeated flat battery
  • !Uneven tyre wear
  • !New smoke, smells or leaks

Keep records and receipts

Keeping service invoices, MOT certificates and repair receipts helps track maintenance history and can improve resale confidence.

It also helps avoid repeating work unnecessarily.
