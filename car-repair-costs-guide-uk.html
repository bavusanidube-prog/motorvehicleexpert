What affects repair cost?

Why garage quotes can vary

  • 1Vehicle make, model and engine type
  • 2Labour time and difficulty of access
  • 3Dealer, specialist or independent labour rates
  • 4Genuine, OEM or budget parts
  • 5Extra faults found during repair
Smart cost decisions

How to save money sensibly

1. Confirm the fault

Avoid replacing parts based on guesswork alone.

2. Compare quotes

Check what parts, labour and VAT are included.

3. Act early

Small faults can become expensive if ignored.

4. Value quality

Cheapest is not always best if poor parts are used.
