Starter Motor Clicking But Not Starting

If your car clicks when turning the key or pressing start but the engine does not crank, the fault is often battery or starter related.

Most Common Causes

Weak battery, corroded terminals, faulty starter motor, bad earth lead, seized engine or ignition switch issue.

Single Click

Often starter solenoid engages but there is not enough power to turn the motor.

Rapid Clicking

Usually points to a flat battery or poor battery connection.

What to check first

Quick checks

Battery terminals

Check for loose or corroded clamps.

Headlights

If lights dim heavily while starting, battery may be weak.

Jump start

If it starts with jump leads, battery or charging system likely involved.
