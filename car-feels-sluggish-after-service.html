Car Feels Sluggish After Service

If your car feels sluggish after a service, it may be caused by an unrelated issue, an air intake connection left loose, incorrect parts fitted or a fault that became noticeable afterwards.

Quick answer

A car should not normally feel slower after routine servicing. If it does, something may need rechecking.

If the change happened immediately after collection, contact the garage promptly.

Is it serious?

Sometimes minor

Loose hose or sensor issue.

Sometimes urgent

Misfire or warning light present.

Noisy engine?

Stop and inspect.

Turbo diesel?

Check boost hoses.

Petrol hesitation?

Ignition issue possible.

New symptoms

Worth reporting quickly.

Common causes after a service

  • 1Air filter housing not sealed correctly
  • 2Sensor connector loose
  • 3Vacuum hose disturbed
  • 4Old fault now more noticeable
  • 5Warning code requiring diagnostics

What to check first

Look for warning lights, unusual noises, rough idle and poor acceleration. Note exactly when the sluggishness happens.

Returning to the servicing garage is often the fastest route.
