The diagnostic app is designed to help you understand possible causes and next checks. It does not replace a physical inspection, professional diagnostic scan or qualified mechanic.

Diagnostic App Questions

Is the diagnostic app free?

Yes. The app is free to open and use in your web browser.

Can it tell me if my car is safe to drive?

It gives general safe-to-drive guidance based on the symptom or warning light selected. If the issue feels serious, get the car checked before driving further.

Can it help with MOT problems?

Yes. The app includes MOT-aware guidance for warning lights and faults that may affect brakes, emissions, ABS, airbags, tyres and other testable items.
