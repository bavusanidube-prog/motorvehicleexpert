FAQ

Diagnostic App Questions

Is the diagnostic app free?

Yes. The Motor Vehicle Expert diagnostic app is free to open and use in your web browser.

Can it tell me if my car is safe to drive?

It gives general safe-to-drive guidance. If the problem feels serious, stop safely and get the car checked before driving further.

Can it check OBD codes?

Yes. It gives guidance for common OBD fault codes and related fault areas, but it should not replace professional diagnosis.

Can it help with MOT problems?

Yes. It includes MOT-aware guidance for warning lights and common faults that may affect an MOT test.

Does it replace a mechanic?

No. It is a guidance tool. A mechanic or diagnostic technician is still needed for proper testing and repair.

Should I clear fault codes myself?

Clearing codes without fixing the cause can hide useful information. Always understand the fault first.
