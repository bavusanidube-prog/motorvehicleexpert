Service ESC Light Meaning

A Service ESC light usually means the Electronic Stability Control system has detected a fault and needs diagnosis. Stability or traction assistance may be reduced until repaired.

Quick answer

The car may still drive normally, but ESC support during skids or slippery conditions may not work as intended.

You should arrange diagnosis soon, especially if other warning lights appear.

Is it safe to drive?

Often yes

If the car feels normal.

Reduced stability aid

Less electronic intervention.

Wet roads caution

Drive smoothly and gently.

Multiple lights

Needs faster attention.

Brake symptoms

Stop and inspect urgently.

Temporary faults possible

Low voltage can trigger lights.

Common causes of Service ESC light

  • 1Wheel speed sensor fault
  • 2ABS system issue
  • 3Steering angle sensor fault
  • 4Low battery or charging issue
  • 5Control module communication error

How to fix it

Read diagnostic fault codes first. Many ESC faults are sensor or voltage related and can be pinpointed quickly.

Do not rely only on resetting the light without fixing the cause.
