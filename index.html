What does Motor Vehicle Expert help with?

It helps UK drivers understand warning lights, car symptoms, MOT issues, repair costs, servicing, maintenance and used car checks.

Can I use the site to diagnose my car?

You can use it to understand likely causes and checks, but serious faults still need proper inspection or diagnostic testing.

Does the site cover MOT failures?

Yes. It includes MOT failure guides for tyres, brakes, lights, warning lights, rust, suspension, steering, wipers and more.

Does the site cover repair costs?

Yes. It covers common UK repair topics including clutches, brake pads, alternators, starter motors, wheel bearings and coolant leaks.
