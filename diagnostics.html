Common symptom categories

What kind of problem are you dealing with?

  • 1Dashboard warning lights and fault messages
  • 2Starting problems and battery-related faults
  • 3Brake noises, poor pedal feel or unstable braking
  • 4Rough running, misfire, hesitation or loss of power
  • 5Vibration, shaking, odd noises or drivability issues
Good diagnostic habits

How to approach a fault more sensibly

1. Notice the symptom

Pay attention to what the car is doing, when it happens and whether a warning light is involved.

2. Avoid guessing

Do not replace parts at random just because they are commonly blamed online.

3. Judge urgency

Some issues are inconvenient, while others make the vehicle unsafe or unsuitable to keep driving.

4. Confirm the cause

Use proper checks, fault-code reading and inspection before spending money on repairs.
