Common symptom categories

What kind of problem are you dealing with?

  • 1Dashboard warning lights and fault messages.
  • 2Starting problems, battery faults and charging issues.
  • 3Coolant loss, overheating, smoke or engine temperature changes.
  • 4Rough running, misfire, hesitation, jerking or loss of power.
  • 5Vibration, shaking, pulling, braking problems or unusual smells.
Good diagnostic habits

How to approach a fault sensibly

1. Notice the pattern

When does it happen: cold, hot, idle, braking, accelerating, uphill, traffic or motorway speed?

2. Avoid guessing

Do not replace parts at random just because they are commonly blamed online.

3. Judge urgency

Coolant, oil, brake, steering, smoke and overheating faults can become unsafe quickly.

4. Confirm the cause

Use proper checks, fault-code reading and inspection before spending money.
