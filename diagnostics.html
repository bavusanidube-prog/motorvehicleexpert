Best article format

How to structure diagnostic articles

1. State the symptom

Describe what the driver is seeing, hearing or feeling.

2. Explain likely causes

List the most realistic possibilities without overpromising certainty.

3. Explain the risk

Tell readers whether it is safe to drive or whether inspection is urgent.

4. Explain next checks

Offer sensible next steps such as scans, visual checks or workshop diagnosis.
